About Us

Introduction

We are glad to introduce ourselves as leaders in processing frozen fruits and vegetables.

Specializing in developing, growing and processing all fruits and vegetables, milk products, at blast freezing, food and many more.

Shree Dauji Sudarshan Sheetgrah, established in the year 2023 & headed by
Mr. Alok Kaushik , the stalwart with more than two decades of experience in frozen food industry, situated at Bhagwati Dharam Kanta, Mursan- Sadabad Road, Bamnai (UttarPradesh, India).

Under the guidance of Mr. Alok Kaushik, we have been able to survive in this competitive market. His leadership quality, excellent skills and ethical business strategies have enabled us to set new benchmarks in the industry.

Our decade’s long experience and finest infrastructure have aided us to earn recognition as one of the leaders in this field which in turn enables us to work with extremely reputed big names in the various industries.

Our success is an outcome of strategic focus & constantly investing in state of the art infrastructure & a team of experienced experts, which delivers utmost hygiene, safety & unmatchable services.

We are engaged in continuous improvement of our services to enhance the quality and cost competitiveness in order to build value for our customers.

It is our immense pleasure to say that we are client centric organization. We are totally committed to our clients and willingness to deliver a flexible, customer orientated approach to meet the individual needs of its business.

It is our endeavour to team up with small and large companies alike to provide healthy, superior and affordable services to meet the demands of the market.

Methodology

Being the oldest civilization in the world, India prides of tradition, customs, and food culture as old as its people. To be able to capture this very culture in our products, we conducted countless man hours of research into food habits and culture of years past.

We merged our research results with modern technology to preserve the freshness in food and of course the tradition and the authenticity too. What’s more, our commitment to highest quality standards ensures that we employ state-of-the-art production technology and adhere to the stringent international quality control standards.

ISO 9001:2015 obtaining these certifications is only a recognition of our commitment to our continuous improvement both in terms of product quality and customer service.
